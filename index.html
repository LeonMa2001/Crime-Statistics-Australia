Healthsites Of Australia

Healthsite Mapped on Australia

This map visualisation initially illustrates all of the accumulated data points of healthsites listed in the dataset. It plots it onto the map of Australia, which also includes filters to be able to search for specific healthsite types, as well as filter between the centred state.


  • The select Amenity option allows filtering between amenities healthsites specialize in, with said options being: Doctors, Clinics, Pharmacies, Hospitals, and unspecified.
  • The Zoom option allows zooming of the map to look further or closer to the selected centre.
  • The Map Centre option allows toggling between all different states of Australia, allowing an interactive toggle on which area is focussed on, working in conjunction with the Zoom option.
  • To reset the view to Australia, change zoom to 1000 (min) and set the Map Centre option to "Australia".

Health Conditions and Factors by state

Health Conditions by state

This graph shows the proportion of people in Australia with various long-term health conditions by state. Users can choose a specific condition from a dropdown, updating the chart to display the percentage affected in each state. This helps compare how health conditions vary regionally, highlighting trends and disparities. The national average is also shown for broader context.

Health Factors by state

This graph displays the percentage of adults (18+) with health risk factors across Australian states, such as smoking or dietary habits. Each bar represents a different risk factor, enabling comparison between states and against the national average. It highlights key public health concerns and regional differences.
